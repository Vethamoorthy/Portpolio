About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!

  • My name is Vedamurthy Ayyachamy, and I am a passionate and dedicated GIS Analyst with over 25 years of professional experience across the UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and India. I hold a BSc in Computer Science and a MSc in Geoinformatics, which have provided a solid foundation for my successful career in geospatial technology and data analysis.

  • I take pride in combining technical expertise with real-world applications, contributing to major infrastructure and urban planning projects. My work is driven by accuracy, innovation, and a deep interest in spatial intelligence and data-driven decision-making.

My Hobbies:

  • Outside of work, I enjoy spending my time in creative and thoughtful ways. Some of my favorite hobbies include:
  • Exploring new geospatial technologies and web mapping tools
  • Photography, especially landscapes and cultural sites
  • Traveling and learning about different cultures and places
  • Reading technical articles and self-improvement books
  • Volunteering for community mapping and GIS awareness programs
  • These hobbies not only enrich my personal life but also help me stay inspired and innovative in my professional journey.

Work Experience

  • Over the past two and a half decades, I’ve had the privilege to work on a wide variety of GIS and mapping projects with reputed organizations. Some highlights include:
  • GIS Analyst at various leading firms across the UAE, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and India
  • Successful involvement in the FRP4 Project for Ashghal Qatar (2020–2023) while working with Dorsch Qatar
  • Development and management of ArcGIS Portals, Dashboards, and Web Maps
  • Specialization in Lidar, Remote Sensing, Planimetry, and Photogrammetry
  • Experience in GIS data quality control, spatial analysis, and map production
  • My work has consistently supported smart infrastructure, urban planning, and public services through high-quality spatial data and GIS platforms.

Skills & Tools

  • Here are some of the key skills and tools I bring to the table:
  • GIS Software: ArcGIS Desktop, ArcGIS Pro, QGIS
  • Web GIS: ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS Portal, Dashboard & Web AppBuilder
  • Programming & Scripting: HTML, JavaScript, Python (basic for GIS automation)
  • Remote Sensing & Photogrammetry: Lidar, UAV Imagery, ERDAS Imagine
  • Data Capture & Management: Planimetry, Geodatabase creation, Data QC
  • Others: CAD to GIS conversion, shapefile editing, GPS data processing
